After the flower bath

petals caught in hair

on the drain cover.

He misses her,

corsage saver

keeper

of children's artwork,

whose blue-holed flesh

is used to parting

with sacrifices

to boys' trials

and men's desires,

and wonders now

what there was

to be afraid of.
