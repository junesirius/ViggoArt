It's a long, long way

made of stone

from Omaha to Cairo.

From the riverbank,

through the dying fields;

across the sea,

to the bloody quarries

where they keep digging

for rulers

while school bells

echo

north and south

off the twin brown

mirrors of the Missouri

and the Nile.

(1992)
