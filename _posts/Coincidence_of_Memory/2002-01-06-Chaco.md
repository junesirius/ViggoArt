Me cago en la selva,

como los monos

con sus dientes

perfectos y amarillos,

sin tenerle miedo

a ningun tigre.

(1995)

<br><br>

(Self-translation:)

I shit in the jungle.

like monkeys

with their teeth

perfect and yellow

without being afraid of

any tiger.
